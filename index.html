About CityBlocks

CityBlocks is a creative collaboration platform built on a premium domain owned since 1997.

Our mission: Create the first TV-optimized platform where creators collaborate in living brownstone communities.

Algorithm-free. No AI deciding who gets seen.

Politics-free. Pure creativity, zero controversy.

Community-first. Content that amplifies everyone's reach through collaboration.

How It Works

Step 1: Creators Upload to Library

Submit 30-second clips tagged by theme, mood, or style. One upload can be featured in hundreds of brownstones!

Step 2: Hosts Build Brownstones

Browse the library and select 12 videos that work together. Create comedy brownstones, drama brownstones, musical brownstones - anything!

Step 3: Visitors Watch Performances

Choose Sequential (story) or Random (chaos). Same 12 videos create infinite unique performances!

The Collaboration Library

The heart of CityBlocks is the Collaboration Library - a searchable collection of creator content designed for mixing and remixing.

Submit once, featured everywhere. Your drummer beat could be used in 100 different brownstones. That's 100x the exposure!

Network effects mean the more content in the library, the more creative combinations possible, and the more exposure for everyone.

Phase 2 will launch the full browsable library. For now, enjoy the proof of concept!
