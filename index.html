Host Window

The Host is the project creator who welcomes collaborators into their creative brownstone.

As the host, you'll define your project, set the vision, and invite up to 12 collaborators from different creative disciplines to bring your idea to life.

Think of yourself as the director of your creative team - you're building something amazing together!

About CityBlocks

CityBlocks is a creative collaboration platform where artists, musicians, filmmakers, and creators of all kinds come together to build amazing collaborative video projects.

Each creator gets their own customizable brownstone with 13 windows - one for the host and twelve for collaborators. It's like Hollywood Squares meets creative collaboration!

Algorithm-free. Politics-free. Pure creativity.
