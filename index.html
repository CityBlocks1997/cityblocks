About CityBlocks

Welcome to CityBlocks.com - where creativity meets community in a neighborhood built by creators, for creators.

üè¢ The Vision: Inspired by iconic urban architecture, CityBlocks gives every creator their own digital brownstone. Just like the diverse, vibrant buildings that line city streets, each brownstone on CityBlocks reflects the unique personality and creative vision of its owner.

üé® How It Works:

  • Customize Your Brownstone: Choose colors, patterns, or upload your own designs. From solid colors to psychedelic 60s patterns, street art graffiti, or pride rainbows - make it yours.
  • One Host Window: Your featured work takes center stage in your brownstone's main window.
  • 12 Collaborator Windows: Connect with other creators, showcase collaborations, or feature work you admire.
  • Build Your Neighborhood: As more creators join, the directory becomes a beautiful mosaic of digital brownstones - each one unique.

ü§ù Our Principles:

  • Algorithm-Free: No mysterious algorithms deciding who sees your work. Your connections are yours.
  • Politics-Free: This is about creativity and collaboration, not controversy.
  • Creator-Focused: Every feature is designed to help creators connect and collaborate.
  • Community First: Personalization is free. Building community is the primary goal.

üéØ The Dream: We believe in the power of creative connection. Maybe a musician finds an animator. Maybe a writer meets an illustrator. Maybe the next great collaboration starts right here in your neighborhood.

üìÖ Established: Domain registered in 1997, reimagined for 2025 as a platform for the next generation of creators.
