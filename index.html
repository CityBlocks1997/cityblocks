About CityBlocks

Welcome to CityBlocks.com - where creativity meets community through collaborative video storytelling.

üè¢ The Brownstone Concept: Inspired by iconic urban architecture and the Hollywood Squares, each brownstone becomes an interactive video canvas with 13 windows - one host and twelve collaborators.

üé¨ How It Works:

  • Host Creates a Project: Set your creative goal (animation, music video, comedy sketch, etc.)
  • Creators Submit Videos: Musicians, artists, animators, and other creators submit their contributions
  • Host Approves Content: Review submissions and approve the best ones
  • Videos Populate Your Brownstone: Approved videos appear in your 12 collaborator windows
  • Playback Options: Sequential for narratives/cartoons or random for comedy Ad-Libs effect

üé® Customize Your Brownstone:

  • Solid Colors: Classic brick tones or bold modern hues
  • Repeating Patterns: Psychedelic 60s designs, flag patterns, artistic motifs
  • Custom Images: Upload your own designs for maximum personalization
  • Your Brand: Make your brownstone instantly recognizable

ü§ù Our Principles:

  • Algorithm-Free: No AI deciding who sees your work. Your project, your collaborators.
  • Politics-Free: Pure creativity without controversy.
  • Creator-Focused: Tools designed for actual collaboration, not just content consumption.
  • Community First: Personalization is free. Building connections is the goal.

üéØ The Vision: CityBlocks is Hollywood Squares meets TikTok Duets meets collaborative storytelling. Create projects that bring together musicians, animators, voice artists, and more - all in one beautiful brownstone interface.

üìÖ Established: Premium domain since 1997, reimagined for 2025 as the ultimate creative collaboration platform.
