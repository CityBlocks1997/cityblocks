About CityBlocks

CityBlocks is a creative collaboration platform built on a premium domain owned since 1997.

Algorithm-free. No AI deciding who gets seen.

Politics-free. Pure creativity, zero controversy.

Community-first. Content that amplifies everyone's reach through collaboration.

How It Works

Step 1: Creators Upload to Library

Submit 30-second clips tagged by theme, mood, or style.

Step 2: Hosts Build Brownstones

Browse the library and select 12 videos that work together.

Step 3: Watch Performances

Sequential (story), Random (chaos), or Loop/Layer (live mix)!
