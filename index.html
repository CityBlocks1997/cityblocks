About CityBlocks

Welcome to CityBlocks.com - where creativity meets community through collaborative video projects.

üè¢ The Brownstone Concept: Inspired by iconic urban architecture, each brownstone is an interactive video canvas with 13 windows - one host and twelve collaborators.

üé¨ How It Works:

  • Host Creates Project: Set your creative goal (animation, music video, comedy, etc.)
  • Creators Submit Videos: From 12 categories submit contributions
  • Host Approves: Review and approve the best submissions
  • Videos Populate Windows: Approved videos fill your 12 collaborator windows
  • Playback Options: Sequential for narratives or random for comedy

üé® Customize Your Brownstone:

  • Solid colors, repeating patterns, or custom image uploads
  • Make your brownstone uniquely yours

ü§ù Our Principles:

  • Algorithm-Free: No AI deciding visibility
  • Politics-Free: Pure creativity
  • Creator-Focused: Tools for real collaboration
